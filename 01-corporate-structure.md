# 01 · Corporate structure

The single most important thing to get right, because almost every confusing headline collapses onto it.

## The hierarchy (as of June 2026)

SpaceX is now the **parent** that absorbed xAI, not a sibling. Two moves built it:

1. **2025-03-28** `[HIGH]`: xAI acquired X Corp (the old Twitter) in an all-stock deal, valuing xAI at ~$80B and X at ~$33B. X and Grok folded into xAI. (Sullivan & Cromwell, deal counsel.)
2. **2026-02-02** `[HIGH]`: **SpaceX acquired xAI** in an all-stock deal: SpaceX valued ~$1T, xAI ~$250B, combined **~$1.25 trillion** (the largest merger ever recorded). Exchange ratio 0.1433 SpaceX shares per xAI share (xAI $75.46, SpaceX $526.59). xAI became a wholly owned SpaceX subsidiary; X and Grok came along inside it. (CNBC, Bloomberg, Fortune, TechCrunch; SpaceX S-1.)

By **May 2026** `[HIGH]`, Musk said xAI would cease to exist as a standalone company, with Grok and X operating as a SpaceX AI division branded **"SpaceXAI."** The S-1 reports the consolidated company across three segments: **Space, Connectivity (Starlink), and AI.**

```
                     SpaceX  (Nasdaq: SPCX)
                     ├── Space          (Falcon, Starship, Starshield)
                     ├── Connectivity   (Starlink, Direct-to-Cell)
                     └── AI  (ex-xAI)
                          ├── Grok
                          ├── Colossus 1 & 2 (Memphis-area datacenters)
                          ├── X (social platform)
                          └── [pending] Anysphere / Cursor  (close ~Q3 2026)
```

## What is NOT inside SpaceX

- **Tesla**: separate public company. Its only tie is a **~$2B investment into xAI** (agreed 2026-01-16, closed ~2026-03-12) that converted into SpaceX stock post-merger. `[HIGH]`
- **Anysphere (Cursor)**: separate company, currently being *acquired* by SpaceX ($60B all-stock, announced 2026-06-16, close ~Q3 2026). Not yet closed. See [05-cursor-anysphere.md](05-cursor-anysphere.md). `[HIGH]`
- **"Macrohard"**: NOT a company you can point to. It is an xAI software venture/trademark (X.AI LLC, USPTO filed 2025-08-01), reframed in March 2026 as a joint Tesla-xAI project ("Digital Optimus"). Do not confuse it with the **MACROHARDRR** data-center building (see [03-ai-compute.md](03-ai-compute.md)). `[HIGH]`

## Two separate ~$2B investments people conflate

- **SpaceX → xAI ~$2B** (reported July 2025), pre-merger. `[MED]`
- **Tesla → xAI ~$2B** Series E preferred (Jan 2026, closed March 2026). `[HIGH]`

These are different. Tesla shareholders had *rejected* a separate proposal to authorize up to $5B into xAI at the Nov 2025 annual meeting; the January deal proceeded under prior board authority.

## Ownership & control

- Musk holds ~40-42% of SpaceX equity post-IPO but **~82.4% of voting power** via dual-class shares (Class B = 10 votes). SpaceX is a controlled company. `[HIGH]`
- Musk's SpaceX **compensation package** is reportedly structured around valuation milestones, the first award vesting at a **$7.5 trillion** valuation. Source: a16z article "SpaceX & the Sentient Sun" (@pmarca, @mikemcg0, Jun 15 2026). `[MED]`

## Why this matters for the rest of the brief

- "Anthropic/Google rent Colossus" → they rent from **SpaceX** (owner of the ex-xAI datacenters).
- "Cursor acquisition" → the acquirer is **SpaceX**, because SpaceX owns the AI business now.
- "xAI did X" in any 2026 headline → read it as **SpaceX**.
